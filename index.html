Pyramid International School - The Foundation of Success

"The foundation of success begins with the right school!" Pyramid International School is a CBSE-affiliated institution (Affiliation No. 1131240) dedicated to providing holistic education that nurtures every child's intellectual, emotional, and social growth. Our philosophy is built on five pillars: knowledge, wisdom, spiritual perception, eloquent speech, and vision.

We emphasize experiential learning and all-round development. Located on a 2-acre eco-friendly campus in Silicon Valley, Behind Himayat Bagh, Aurangabad, we offer world-class infrastructure including smart classrooms, well-equipped science and computer labs, air-conditioned learning spaces, and comprehensive sports facilities.

Our dynamic faculty and student-centered approach ensure students are prepared for the competitive 21st-century world while maintaining strong values and character. We provide specialized coaching for competitive exams like NDA, JEE Main, and NEET, ensuring comprehensive academic excellence.

With 14+ years of excellence in education, 50+ dedicated faculty members, 25+ smart classrooms, and 1000+ happy students, we continue to shape future leaders who excel academically while being grounded in values and ethics.
