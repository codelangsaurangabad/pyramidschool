Pyramid International School - The Foundation of Success

"The foundation of success begins with the right school!" Pyramid International School is a CBSE-affiliated institution (Affiliation No. 1131240) dedicated to providing holistic education that nurtures every child's intellectual, emotional, and social growth. Our philosophy is built on five pillars: knowledge, wisdom, spiritual perception, eloquent speech, and vision.

Under the dynamic leadership of Principal Miss. Khan Almas Bakhtiyar Khan, we emphasize experiential learning and all-round development. Located on a 2-acre eco-friendly campus in Silicon Valley, Behind Himayat Bagh, Aurangabad, we offer world-class infrastructure including smart classrooms, well-equipped science and computer labs, air-conditioned learning spaces, and comprehensive sports facilities.

Dear Parents and Students,

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Pyramid International School, where we believe in holistic education that goes beyond textbooks. Since our establishment in 2010, we have been committed to fostering an environment where every child can discover their potential and excel in their chosen path.

At Pyramid, we emphasize a balanced approach to education, combining academic excellence with extracurricular activities, sports, and cultural programs. Our CBSE-affiliated curriculum, coupled with modern teaching methodologies and state-of-the-art infrastructure, ensures that our students receive the best possible foundation for their future.

Our dedicated team of over 50 experienced educators works tirelessly to create an atmosphere of learning, innovation, and creativity. We take pride in our smart classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, extensive library, and eco-friendly campus spread across 2 acres.

I invite you to join our Pyramid family, where we nurture young minds to become confident, compassionate, and capable individuals ready to face the challenges of tomorrow.

Warm Regards,
